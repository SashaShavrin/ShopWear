Classic shoes are made of leather and tied with laces. However, variations in shape, material and method of tying are possible. Modern shoes often use a zipper instead of laces (or together with them). In addition, some shoes (for example, Chelsea) do not have lacing at all, but instead use two rubber inserts on the sides.
Each season has its own shoes. Usually boots are used in winter (to protect from snow), spring and autumn (to protect from dirt and deep puddles). There are also special boots, such as ski boots or tactical boots.
